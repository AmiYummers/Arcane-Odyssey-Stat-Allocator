Arcane Odyssey Imbue Calculator

Pick your Class

This is a simple imbue calculator made by chatgpt and Yummers/AmyaUri.

Here, you can calculate the stats of stuff that can be imbued, using simple math that can be found on balance discord of AO and all of its related sites. You can go into calculator's code to look at the values and point out where they are wrong.

This generator has all hybrids that can imbue, including the vitality ones.

Notes

  1. Some magics and fighting styles have unique damage affinities when they are imbuing ONTO something.
  2. All spirit imbues are calculated as 1x 1x 1x, except for Knight, who gets 11.11% more size.
